Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

split it. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • Expense Data: Information about groups you create, expenses you add, and settlements you record.
  • Device Information: We may collect device identifiers and general usage statistics to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and track your expenses and settlements
  • Send you notifications about your groups and balances
  • Display relevant advertisements to support our free service
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests

Advertising

split it. is a free, ad-supported application. We work with third-party advertising partners to display ads within the app. These partners may collect and use certain data about your device and app usage to show you relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase as our backend provider, which employs robust security measures to protect your information. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other members of groups you join (limited to expense-related data)
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your expense data

Children's Privacy

split it. is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
